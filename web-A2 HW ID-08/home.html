Grand Canyon

Thunder, Jan 7, 2022

Arizona

The Grand Canyon in Arizona is a natural formation distinguished by layered bands of red rock,
revealing millions of years of geological history in cross-section. Vast in scale, the canyon
averages 10 miles across and a mile deep along its 277-mile length.

Himalayas

Mount Everest, Jan 17, 2022

Asia

The Himalayas, or Himalaya, are a mountain range in Asia separating the plains of the Indian subcontinent
from the Tibetan Plateau. The range has some of the planet's highest peaks,
including the highest, Mount Everest.
